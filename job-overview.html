About the Role

As a Team Coach, you will be responsible for mentoring and guiding team members to achieve their best performance. Your role involves setting goals, evaluating team progress, and providing constructive feedback to ensure the success of the team.

Additional responsibilities include developing training programs, fostering a collaborative work environment, and assisting in the resolution of conflicts to maintain team harmony.
